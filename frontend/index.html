Smart Photo Album

This is an app that allows you to upload your photos into a smart album. Labels are automatically detected within your image using AWS Rekognition and stored in an ElasticSearch instance for quick/dynamic photo lookups. Or you can perform the search with your voice using AWS Transcribe to transcribe speach to text in real-time.
